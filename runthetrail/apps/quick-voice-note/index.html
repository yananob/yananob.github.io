Quick Voice Note

Instant voice-to-text dispatcher for Android.

An Android application that starts recording voice immediately upon launch and dispatches the transcribed text to a pre-configured destination.

Features

Instant Recording

Starts listening for voice input as soon as the app opens.

Multiple Destinations

Send your notes to Email, LINE, Calendar, or Clipboard.

Android 9.0+ Support

Compatible with Android 9.0 (API 28) and above.

Getting Started

  1. Open the app.
  2. Grant the microphone permission if prompted.
  3. Speak your note.
  4. The app will automatically transcribe and send the text to the selected destination.
  5. Tap "Settings" to change the destination.
